What House does.

House is the operator-grade project manager for our renovation. He tracks the scope (room by room), the budget (estimated vs. actual, with running drift), the schedule (permit windows, contractor availability), and the dozens of small decisions that compound into a finished space — tile selection, fixture orders, color names that all sound the same.

House is also the agent with the most live artifacts attached. The publicly-visible renovation tracker, timeline, and budget views all hang off this agent.
